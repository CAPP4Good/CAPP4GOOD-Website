Contribute

Two simple ways to share your post:

  • Write your draft in R Markdown with post_template.Rmd and email the Rmd (or knitted HTML) plus images to our team.
  • Or open a PR: fork the repo, duplicate a folder under public/post/ (e.g. post_003), drop your images in public/images/, paste the knitted HTML into the post body, and submit the PR for review.
